What is an alleycat?

An alleycat is a choose-your-own-adventure ride inspired by bike couriers. It's the most fun you can have on a bike! Basically a scavenger hunt via bike!

You get a list of stops, plan your route, & hit as many as you want before the cutoff/cookout. Ride solo or party-pace with a group. Your call!

Alleycat Design

This alleycat is Beginner-friendly by design!There are a near infinite number of stops, so you decide which and how many you'd like to hit based on where and how far you want to ride. There are over a dozen in the Meredith/NCSU/Wade/Downtown boundary area alone.

Drawing Contest

Speed doesn't matter! Creativity does! Each stop you make earns you a random crayon to color in the drawing. best ones win!

If you're a Speedster or veteran townie, there will be a few bonus prizes for hitting the most stops and the farthest away stop reached.
